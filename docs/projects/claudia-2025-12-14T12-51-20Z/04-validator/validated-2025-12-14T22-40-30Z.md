## Visual Style Guide

### Aesthetic Zone (Scenes 1-2)
- **Color Palette:** Warm golden hour, soft pastels, RGB accents
- **Lighting:** Artificial golden hour, soft and flattering
- **Texture:** Clean, pristine, dust-free surfaces
- **Mood:** Uncanny perfection, slightly unsettling

### Reality Zone (Scenes 3-9)
- **Color Palette:** Cool fluorescent whites, natural tones, no color grading
- **Lighting:** Harsh overhead fluorescent, unflattering but honest
- **Texture:** Coffee rings, fingerprints, tangled cables, worn surfaces
- **Mood:** Authentic, relatable, liberating imperfection

### Transition (Scene 3)
- **Visual:** Rapid zoom out reveal
- **Audio:** Glitch/stutter breaking the aesthetic
- **Color:** Warm zone shrinks, cold reality expands

### Character Consistency
- Late 20s, casual hoodie, messy uncombed hair
- Tired but knowing expression
- Natural, unperformed movements
- Same person throughout all scenes
