## Component 1: Subject Examples

### Simple Subjects
**Single Object:**
- "A ceramic coffee mug with steam rising"
- "Vintage bicycle leaning against brick wall"
- "Red balloon floating upward"
- "Open book with pages turning"

**Single Person:**
- "Young man in his 30s"
- "Elderly woman with silver hair"
- "Child around 8 years old"
- "Athlete in mid-stride"

### Complex Subjects
**Detailed Characters:**
- "A weathered fisherman in his 60s with salt-and-pepper beard, wearing yellow rain slicker and rubber boots"
- "Ballet dancer in red tutu, hair in tight bun, poised on toes"
- "Chef in white uniform, tattoos visible on forearms, flour dusting apron"
- "Street musician with worn guitar case, fedora tilted back"

**Multiple Subjects:**
- "Pair of ballet dancers - principal in red tutu, partner in black tights"
- "Group of five children playing, ages 6-10, mixed ethnicities"
- "Three business professionals in suits at conference table"
- "Family of four: parents mid-40s, teenagers 15 and 17"

### Abstract Subjects
**Ephemeral Elements:**
- "Swirling particles of dust in beam of light"
- "Steam rising from hot pavement after rain"
- "Shadow of figure moving across wall"
- "Reflection in puddle showing inverted cityscape"

**Conceptual Subjects:**
- "Abstract patterns of light refracting through crystal"
- "Ink dispersing in water, tendrils spreading"
- "Smoke curling upward in defined stream"

## Component 2: Setting Examples

### Time Specifications
**Precise Timing:**
- "Golden hour, 6:45 PM, sun low on horizon"
- "Midnight, full moon overhead, stars visible"
- "Overcast afternoon, 2:30 PM, diffused light"
- "Dawn, 5:15 AM, first light breaking"
- "Blue hour, 8:10 PM, twilight deepening"

**General Time Periods:**
- "Early morning, dew still on grass"
- "High noon, sun directly overhead"
- "Late evening, streetlights just coming on"
- "Deep night, only artificial light sources"

### Location Specificity
**Detailed Locations:**
- "Industrial warehouse with exposed brick, steel I-beams, concrete floor with oil stains"
- "Cozy independent bookshop corner with leather armchair, floor lamp, stacks of hardcovers"
- "Desert highway, heat shimmer visible, distant mountains purple against sky"
- "Subway platform, fluorescent lights humming, yellow safety line, tile walls with vintage ads"

**Atmospheric Settings:**
- "Rain-soaked cobblestone street reflecting neon signs"
- "Sun-dappled forest path, light filtering through canopy"
- "Fog-shrouded pier, visibility limited to 20 feet"
- "Snow-covered field, pristine and unmarked"

### Spatial Anchors (Foreground/Midground/Background)
**Three-Layer Composition:**
```
Foreground: Raindrops on window glass, creating bokeh
Midground: Couple at café table, faces close
Background: Blurred café interior, warm Edison bulbs, other patrons indistinct
```

```
Foreground: Wheat stalks in sharp focus, swaying
Midground: Farmer walking through field, silhouette
Background: Red barn, silo, distant tree line at sunset
```

```
Foreground: Car hood ornament gleaming
Midground: Classic car, chrome bumper, white-wall tires
Background: 1950s diner with neon sign, parking lot
```

## Component 3: Action Examples

### Simple Actions
**Single Movement:**
- "Pours coffee from French press into white mug"
- "Turns page of leather-bound book"
- "Walks slowly toward camera, hands in pockets"
- "Closes eyes, takes deep breath"

### Complex Actions
**Multi-Step Sequences:**
- "Opens umbrella, shakes off water droplets, looks up at sky, smiles slightly"
- "Picks up phone, reads message, expression changes from neutral to surprised, sets phone down slowly"
- "Strikes match, watches flame grow, brings it to candle wick, shakes out match"

**Emotional Actions:**
- "Hesitates at doorway, hand on knob, looks back over shoulder, then exits"
- "Receives news, processes information silently, tears well up, tries to smile through them"
- "Arguments escalating - gestures become larger, voices rising (inferred), then sudden silence"

### Continuous Actions
**Ongoing Processes:**
- "Traffic flows continuously through intersection, brake lights and headlights creating light trails"
- "Waves crash rhythmically against rocks, spray catching light"
- "Crowd mills about, individuals crossing frame in different directions"

## Component 5: Camera/Composition Examples

### Shot Sizes
**Wide Shots:**
- "Extreme wide shot, subject small in vast landscape"
- "Wide shot, full body visible with environmental context"
- "Medium wide, showing subject waist-up with setting visible"

**Close Shots:**
- "Medium close-up, head and shoulders framed"
- "Close-up, face filling frame"
- "Extreme close-up, eyes only, detail visible in iris"

### Angles
**Height Variations:**
- "Eye level, neutral perspective"
- "Low angle, camera looking up, subject appears powerful"
- "High angle, looking down, subject appears vulnerable"
- "Bird's eye view, directly overhead"
- "Worm's eye view, directly below looking up"

**Perspective Variations:**
- "Over-shoulder shot, viewer positioned behind subject"
- "POV shot, seeing exactly what subject sees"
- "Dutch angle (tilted), 15 degrees off horizontal for unease"

### Composition Techniques
**Rule of Thirds:**
- "Subject positioned at right third intersection, facing left, negative space on left for eye line"

**Leading Lines:**
- "Railroad tracks converging toward horizon, subject walking along ties"

**Framing:**
- "Subject framed by doorway, darkness surrounding, light illuminating from behind"

## Component 6: Lighting/Mood Examples

### Natural Light
**Time-Based:**
- "Golden hour: warm, amber tones, long soft shadows, sun at 15-degree angle"
- "Blue hour: cool blue tones, twilight glow, minimal shadows"
- "Overcast: soft, even light, no harsh shadows, slightly desaturated"
- "High noon: harsh overhead light, strong shadows, high contrast"

### Artificial Light Sources
**Practical Lights:**
- "Single desk lamp, warm tungsten glow, pool of light on workspace, shadows falling off quickly"
- "Neon signs: pink and blue neon reflecting in puddles, harsh colored light"
- "Candlelight: warm, flickering, intimate, shadows dancing on walls"
- "Fluorescent: cool, slightly green cast, even and unflattering"

### Lighting Setups
**Three-Point Lighting:**
- "Key light from camera left at 45 degrees, fill from right at lower intensity, hair light from behind"

**Dramatic Lighting:**
- "Single source side lighting, half face illuminated, half in shadow (split lighting)"
- "Backlighting: subject silhouetted, rim light defining edges"
- "Bottom lighting: upward light creating eerie shadows (horror convention)"

### Mood Through Color
**Warm Palette:**
- "Amber gold, burnt orange, deep red - cozy, intimate, nostalgic"

**Cool Palette:**
- "Teal, navy blue, slate gray - melancholy, professional, distant"

**Contrasting Colors:**
- "Teal shadows with amber highlights (teal-and-orange look)"
- "Cool blue environment with warm practical lights punctuating"

## Component 7: Audio Examples

### Dialogue Variations
**Whispers:**
- `She whispers: "They're coming." (no subtitles)`
- `He breathes: "Wait..." barely audible (no subtitles)`

**Normal Conversation:**
- `Character A: "We should leave now." Character B: "Give it five more minutes."`

**Shouting/Emotional:**
- `She yells: "I said no!" voice echoing (no subtitles)`
- `He chokes out: "I'm sorry." voice breaking (no subtitles)`

### Ambient Sound Layers
**Urban Environments:**
- "Distant traffic hum, car horn in distance, footsteps on pavement, wind rustling trash, subway rumble beneath"

**Nature Environments:**
- "Gentle stream burbling, birds chirping (cardinal and sparrow distinct), leaves rustling, distant woodpecker"

**Interior Spaces:**
- "Clock ticking, refrigerator hum, house settling creak, distant voices muffled through walls"

### Foley Details (Micro-Sounds)
**Specific Actions:**
- "Leather shoe squeak on linoleum"
- "Zipper pulled slowly with metallic rasp"
- "Paper crumpling, fibers tearing"
- "Liquid pouring, gulp of swallow, glass set down with clink"
- "Match strike, sulfur ignition, paper catching flame"

### Musical Elements
**Scoring Descriptions:**
- "Melancholic piano, minor key, sparse notes with long reverb"
- "Driving percussion, building tension, tempo increasing"
- "Ethereal strings, sustained high notes, no distinct melody"
- "Solo acoustic guitar, fingerpicking pattern, intimate"

### Silence as Choice
**Intentional Absence:**
- "Complete silence except single dripping faucet"
- "Muted world, no background ambience, isolated"
- "Sound suddenly cuts out, creating vacuum of silence"

## Component 8: Constraints Examples

### Visual Constraints
**Prohibitions:**
- "(no subtitles)" - most common for clean frames
- "(no visible branding)" - for commercial use
- "(no modern technology visible)" - for period pieces
- "(no people in background)" - isolated subjects

**Exact Requirements:**
- "exactly six candles on the table"
- "precisely three birds in flight"
- "four windows visible, all with curtains drawn"
- "two coffee cups, one half-full, one empty"

### Temporal Constraints
**Duration/Timing:**
- "action completes in exactly 8 seconds"
- "hold for 3 seconds before movement begins"
- "transition must occur at 5-second mark"

### Color Constraints
**Palette Restrictions:**
- "use only warm tones, no blues or greens"
- "monochromatic palette, shades of gray only"
- "single accent color (red) against desaturated background"

### Continuity Constraints
**Consistency Requirements:**
- "maintain wardrobe: blue jacket, white shirt throughout"
- "coffee cup must remain in left hand entire sequence"
- "time of day: dusk, never transitioning to night"

## Complete Prompts by Genre

### Cinematic Drama Example
```
Medium shot, eye level. A woman in her 40s sits alone at restaurant table, 
diamond wedding ring visible as she slowly turns wine glass. Setting: upscale 
restaurant, evening, warm Edison bulb lighting filtering through red wine. 
She glances toward empty chair opposite, expression shifting from hope to 
resignation. Camera: static hold for 3 seconds, then gentle dolly in to 
medium close-up. Lighting: Warm key from camera left (practical lamp), cool 
fill from window behind, rim light catching hair. Color palette: amber gold, 
burgundy red, navy blue, warm ivory skin tones. Audio: Muffled restaurant 
ambience (silverware clinks, distant conversation), her breathing audible 
close-up. (no subtitles)

Style: Cinematic drama with emotional realism, intimate framing.
```

### Documentary Realism Example
```
Handheld, shoulder-mounted, eye level. Street food vendor in his 50s preparing 
pad thai at busy night market stall. Setting: Bangkok street market, 9:30 PM, 
neon signs reflecting in wet pavement. He tosses noodles in wok with practiced 
motion, flames leap up briefly illuminating face, smoke rises. Camera: handheld 
drift right following action, slight jitter natural. Lighting: Mixed sources - 
warm overhead bulb directly above wok, cool blue neon from adjacent stall, 
orange flame glow from burner. Color palette: warm amber, electric blue, orange 
fire, deep shadows. Audio: Sizzling and popping, market chatter layered 
(multiple languages), distant motorbike engines, metal spatula scraping wok. 
Vendor says: "Almost ready!" in Thai-accented English. (no subtitles)

Style: Documentary realism, verité aesthetic, authentic environment.
```

### Neo-Noir Example
```
Low angle, tilted 10 degrees (subtle dutch). Detective in trench coat stands 
in rain-slicked alley, cigarette smoke curling upward. Setting: Urban alley, 
midnight, neon signs from main street casting colored light. He exhales smoke 
slowly, eyes scanning darkness, hand resting on holstered gun beneath coat. 
Camera: static low angle, rain visible in beams of light. Lighting: Single 
street lamp creates pool of light from above, pink and blue neon from behind 
creates rim lighting, wet pavement reflects all sources creating double exposure 
effect. Color palette: deep blacks, electric pink neon, cyan blue, amber 
streetlight, silver rain. Audio: Rain patter on pavement, distant traffic, 
steam hissing from vent, his breathing steady and measured. (no subtitles)

Style: Neo-noir with high-contrast shadows and glowing neon signs.
```

### Horror/Suspense Example
```
Wide shot descending to medium, high angle. Young woman enters dark house, 
only her flashlight beam illuminating space ahead. Setting: Abandoned Victorian 
house, midnight, dust particles visible in beam. She moves cautiously, beam 
sweeping across furniture covered in white sheets, shadows dancing. Camera: 
slow crane down from high angle to eye level as she advances, following beam. 
Lighting: Single practical source (flashlight), extreme contrast, deep blacks 
consuming edges of frame, no fill light. Color palette: desaturated, slight 
blue cast, warm flashlight beam only color. Audio: Floorboards creaking under 
weight, her breathing accelerating, distant sound of something shifting 
upstairs, house settling groan, wind through broken window. She whispers: 
"Hello?" voice trembling. (no subtitles)

Style: Psychological horror, extreme contrast, claustrophobic despite wide shot.
```

### Romantic Comedy Example
```
Medium wide, slightly high angle. Two people collide at coffee shop doorway, 
her coffee splashing (contained, not drenching). Setting: Trendy coffee shop 
entrance, Saturday morning, 10:15 AM, bright natural light. They both freeze, 
eyes meet, simultaneous "I'm sorry!" then awkward laugh. Camera: static for 
collision, quick push-in to medium close-up two-shot for eye contact moment. 
Lighting: Soft natural window light from camera left, bounce from white walls, 
bright and even. Color palette: pastels - soft pink, mint green, cream, warm 
skin tones, optimistic and light. Audio: Coffee shop ambience (espresso machine, 
conversations), collision sound (not aggressive), liquid splash (light), their 
simultaneous apologies creating slight echo, genuine laughs. He says: "Wow, 
great first impression." She replies: "At least your coffee survived." 
(no subtitles)

Style: Romantic comedy, bright and energetic, optimistic tone.
```

### Action Sequence Example
```
Dynamic tracking shot, handheld. Operative runs through crowded market, pursuers 
visible behind. Setting: Middle Eastern bazaar, midday, dusty atmosphere. She 
vaults over cart of melons, causing scatter, ducks under hanging fabrics, 
pushing through crowd. Camera: aggressive handheld tracking, maintaining 
distance, rapid pans to show pursuers. Lighting: Harsh overhead sun creating 
strong shadows, dusty air visible in shafts of light, high contrast. Color 
palette: saturated - deep reds of fabrics, golden dust, vivid blue sky glimpses, 
earth tones. Audio: Heavy breathing, footsteps on dusty ground, crowd shouting 
(Arabic), melons hitting ground and splitting, fabric tearing, pursuers' radio 
chatter distant. (no subtitles)

Style: Action blockbuster, dynamic movement, saturated colors, visceral energy.
```

## Genre Pattern Recognition

### Common Drama Patterns
- Medium shots emphasizing faces
- Warm/neutral lighting
- Subdued audio (intimate, personal sounds)
- Static or gentle camera movements
- Emotional color palettes (warm or melancholic)

### Common Action Patterns
- Dynamic camera movements (handheld, tracking)
- High contrast lighting
- Saturated colors
- Layered, intense audio
- Quick cuts implied through beat descriptions

### Common Horror Patterns
- Extreme contrast (darkness emphasized)
- Desaturated or cool color palette
- Silence punctuated by sudden sounds
- High/low angles for vulnerability/threat
- Negative space and shadows

### Common Romance Patterns
- Soft focus or diffusion
- Warm golden tones
- Gentle movements (slow dolly, crane)
- Intimate audio (breathing, whispers)
- Natural lighting or warm practicals

## Using This Reference

**When creating prompts:**
1. Identify your genre from complete examples
2. Reference individual components for customization
3. Mix patterns from different genres for unique style
4. Validate against 8 mandatory components
5. Ensure audio matches visual tone

**When validating prompts:**
1. Check each component against examples
2. Verify style consistency
3. Confirm audio supports visuals
4. Ensure constraints are clear
5. Cross-reference camera movements with vocabulary

**For inspiration:**
- Browse complete examples by genre
- Study component variations
- Note how audio layers work together
- Observe lighting/mood combinations
- Analyze how constraints shape prompts
